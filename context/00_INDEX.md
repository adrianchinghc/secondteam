## Quick Reference — The Most Important Things to Know

**What we are:** AI-native operating system for owner-led B2B businesses. A product company, not a services company.

**Mission:** "We build the AI operating system that runs the business, so owners can build it."

**BHAG:** $100M ARR in 5 years (by April 2031)

**ICP:** Owner-bottlenecked B2B businesses, $2M–$20M revenue, 5–80 employees, low tech maturity, owner is the buyer

**2026 focus (revised April 14):** Community-first. 70% content/community, 30% product (internal). Phase 1 = content workflow in Cowork + audience build + community setup. Agents come later, built on validated pain points.

**Three-tier sellable ladder:** (1) Workflow templates → (2) Master Company Context tool → (3) Full platform at $799/$1,999/month

**The moat:** Community + Master Company Context Layer + Operator Workflow Library + build-in-public audience + local trust

**Primary distribution:** Build-in-public content flywheel. Adrian posts daily, Jayson posts ≥3x/week. Started April 11. Content IS the Phase 1 product.

**The filter rule:** If a proposal violates any of the 6 Sovereign Business Filter criteria, the answer is automatically no. No exceptions.

**Dead concepts — never suggest these:**
- DFY consulting, live cohorts, facilitated programs
- Human-led sales, human support teams
- Enterprise custom deals
- Batman/Alfred metaphor

- 10-year BHAG (it's 5 years now)
