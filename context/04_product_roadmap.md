## The Master Company Context — Standalone Product Opportunity
This was identified in the April 14 L10 as a significant product in its own right:

**The problem:** 90%+ of AI users don't build a company context file. They just ask questions and get generic answers. With a structured context, every AI interaction becomes dramatically better.

**The product progression:**
1. **Template (now):** Structured markdown file template — free or cheap lead magnet, they fill it in themselves
2. **Tool (Phase 2):** Customer inputs URL, brand guidelines, docs → AI generates the context file for them automatically
3. **Sync product (Phase 3+):** The context stays live and syncs across Claude, ChatGPT, and any AI platform via MCP/API connector — "one source of truth for your AI brain"

**Why this matters strategically:** Every customer who builds a context file becomes a natural candidate for the full Second Team platform. It's the top of the funnel for the product.
