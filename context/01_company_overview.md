# Second Team — Company Overview
*Last updated: April 14, 2026 | V/TO v5 + April 14 L10*

## What We Are
Second Team is building the AI-native operating system for owner-led B2B businesses. Not a consulting firm, not an agency, not a chatbot wrapper — a software **platform** company (confirmed April 14: platform, not just product).

The platform does the work. The founders don't deliver it.

## The One-Line Mission
**"We build the AI operating system that runs the business, so owners can build it."**

Every entrepreneur should be free to focus on the work only they can do — the vision, the relationships, the big calls. Second Team handles everything else: the tools, AI, systems, and knowledge that improve profitability — so owners take more home, spend less time running the business, and more time building it.

## The Niche
**"We sell AI solutions that just work. Whatever you need — we already have it."**

- Software platform, not services. The product does the work.
- Primary users: owner-bottlenecked B2B business owners ($2M–$20M revenue)
- No custom software, no consulting, no cohorts, no enterprise.
